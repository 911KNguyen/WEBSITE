  1. Summary

    Oleksandr Usyk vs Anthony Joshua II, billed as Rage on the Red Sea, was a heavyweight boxing match held on 20 August 2022 in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. Usyk retained his titles by split decision.

  2. Background

    Joshua first lost to Usyk in 2021 in London by unanimous decision. After Russia's invasion of Ukraine, Usyk joined the defense forces but later left to prepare for the rematch.
